Executive Summary

Milsim.AI

Synchronized Multimodal Datasets for AI Training

Date: January 2026 Seed Round: $2.5M

Milsim.AI is building the "Strava for Airsoft"—a community platform for the global milsim/airsoft community that generates premium AI training data as a byproduct of user engagement. By equipping players with a tactical HUD app and leveraging GPS atomic clock synchronization, we capture perfectly-aligned multimodal datasets (video, audio, GPS, sensors) from large-scale tactical operations that are impossible to obtain elsewhere.

The Problem

The $78B+ embodied AI industry faces a critical data bottleneck:

Defense AI companies

Cannot access real military operational data (classified)

Robotics companies

Cannot scale multi-agent data collection (prohibitively expensive)

Simulation companies

Face domain gaps between synthetic and real data

3D/4D AI researchers

Lack synchronized multi-view tactical footage

Current solutions fail: real military exercise data is classified and unavailable, military exercises cost $1-10M+ each, and synthetic data doesn't transfer to real-world performance. Meanwhile, video training data commands $1-4/minute, and AI training data costs have become "a price tag only Big Tech can afford" (TechCrunch).

The Solution

For Airsoft Players

  • Tactical HUD displaying teammate positions, objectives, and communications
  • GPS tracking and post-game replay analytics
  • Event discovery and team management
  • Social features and community engagement

For AI Companies

  • Synchronized multimodal datasets from 100+ participant tactical scenarios
  • Video (4K/60fps) + Audio + GPS + Accelerometer/Gyroscope + Compass
  • Frame-perfect alignment using GPS atomic clock precision (nanoseconds)
  • Ethically sourced with explicit consent
The Innovation

GPS satellites maintain atomic clocks with nanosecond accuracy. At 60fps, each frame spans 16.67ms—well within synchronization tolerance. Our platform embeds GPS timestamps into QR codes displayed on player HUDs, enabling post-hoc verification and perfect multi-device alignment.
